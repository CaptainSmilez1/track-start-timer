Starta — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Short version: Starta does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no network access beyond loading the app itself. Everything you configure stays on your device.

What data we collect

None. Starta does not have servers, does not use analytics or tracking SDKs, and does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other device data.

What's stored on your device

Your preferences — theme, accent color, volume, start-signal sound, and timing ranges — are saved locally on your device (via browser local storage on the web version, or local app storage in the native version) so they persist between sessions. This information never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else.

Sound playback

Starta plays audio start signals locally to time races. No audio is recorded, and no microphone access is used or requested.

Third-party services

Starta does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

Children's privacy

Because Starta collects no personal data from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 or any other age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version adds an optional feature that uses data — this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect that.
